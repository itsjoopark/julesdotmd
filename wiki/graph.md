# 3D Knowledge Graph

An interactive three-dimensional visualization of the wiki's link structure. Each node is a wiki page, sized by how many other pages link to it. Edges represent `[[wikilinks]]` between pages.

**[Open 3D Graph →](../static/graph3d.html)**

- **Navigate**: drag to rotate, scroll to zoom
- **Explore**: hover a node to see its title and tags
- **Dive in**: click a node to open that page

## Color legend

- **Teal** — source pages (`sources/`)
- **Blue** — concept pages (`concepts/`)
- **Purple** — entity pages (`entities/`)
- **Gold** — topic pages (`topics/`)
- **Green** — analysis pages (`analyses/`)
- **Gray** — meta pages (index, log, overview)
